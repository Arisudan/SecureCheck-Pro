Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Autonomy

The SecureCheck Pro Plugin operates with zero data exfiltration. We do not collect your browsing history, analyzed URLs, or code payloads. All intelligence operations are computed locally within the extension's sandbox environment.

2. Plugin Sandbox Storage

All data generated within the "Analysis Hub" is stored locally via your browser's private extension storage APIs.

3. Offline Performance

Our service worker maintains an offline copy of the platform. No network requests are made during the scanning process except to fetch pre-cached static assets, preventing any data leaks via network logs.

4. Founder Access

Not even the Founding Architect (Arisudan) has access to your local "Vault History." Your records are encrypted and stored according to your device's native browser security protocols.

5. Third-Party Requests

We do not share any data with third parties. No advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-site behavioral trackers are active on this domain.
