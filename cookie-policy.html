Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Overview

SecureCheck Pro (Plugin Edition) uses session-only local storage to maintain your audit logs and preferences. Our localized execution model ensures that your data never leaves your environment during a plugin scan.

2. Browser Integration

As a browser extension, we use the chrome.storage and IndexedDB APIs to store your tactical data. This is more efficient than standard web cookies and provides higher security boundaries.

3. Essential Cookies

Some session-level tokens may be used to identify your local instances. These are strictly necessary for the "Offline Mode" and "Sync" functionalities to operate across browser restarts.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party tracking pixels or invasive behavioral analytics. Your interaction with the scanner remains private and local to your client environment.

5. Management

You can clear all locally stored data at any time via the "Vault History" tab using the "Purge Vault" command. This will immediately wipe all session data and preferences.
